# 0.6.5 — 2026-06-21

## Added

- **`ends` on `nws_search_alerts`** — the hazard-end time (ISO 8601) is now mapped and surfaced. `parseAlert` previously dropped the upstream `ends` field; it now flows through the `Alert` type, the tool's output schema, and a `**Hazard ends:**` line in `format()` (paired after `**Hazard onset:**`). Nullable — `null` marks an open-ended hazard ("until further notice"). Distinct from `expires`, which is the CAP message-refresh time (relabeled in [#7](https://github.com/cyanheads/nws-weather-mcp-server/issues/7)) and can fall before `onset` on pre-issued alerts, leaving `ends` as the only structured end-of-hazard signal. ([#18](https://github.com/cyanheads/nws-weather-mcp-server/issues/18))
